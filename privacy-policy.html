Privacy Policy – Keuanganku / MyExpense App

Effective Date: 2025

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal data such as name, email, phone number, or any form of identification. All financial data (expenses, income, savings, categories, and settings) is stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use the Information

Data stored in the App is used solely for:

  • Displaying and calculating financial summaries
  • Budgeting and savings features
  • Analytics and charts inside the App
The App does not share your data with any third parties.

3. Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We implement standard protections, but users are responsible for device security, including backup and unauthorized access prevention.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use Flutter packages such as Shared Preferences (local storage only). These packages do not upload or collect data externally.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for all ages and does not collect personal information from children.

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Any changes will be posted on this page.
